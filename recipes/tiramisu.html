Tiramisu

Description

This is a classic Italian dessert that combines layers of coffee-soaked ladyfingers with a rich mascarpone cheese mixture, topped with cocoa powder.

Ingredients

  • 6 egg yolks
  • 3/4 cup granulated sugar
  • 1 cup mascarpone cheese
  • 1 1/2 cups heavy cream
  • 2 cups brewed espresso or strong coffee, cooled
  • 1/4 cup coffee liqueur (optional)
  • 24 ladyfingers
  • Cocoa powder for dusting

Steps

  1. In a heatproof bowl, whisk together the egg yolks and sugar until well combined.
  2. Place the bowl over a pot of simmering water (double boiler) and cook, whisking constantly, until the mixture thickens and reaches about 160°F (70°C).
  3. Remove from heat and let it cool slightly.
  4. In a separate bowl, beat the mascarpone cheese until smooth.
  5. In another bowl, whip the heavy cream until stiff peaks form.
  6. Gently fold the mascarpone cheese into the egg yolk mixture until well combined.
  7. Fold the whipped cream into the mascarpone mixture until smooth.
  8. In a shallow dish, combine the brewed espresso and coffee liqueur (if using).
  9. Dip each ladyfinger into the coffee mixture for a few seconds, making sure not to soak them too long.
  10. Arrange a layer of soaked ladyfingers in the bottom of a serving dish.
  11. Spread half of the mascarpone mixture over the ladyfingers.
  12. Repeat the layers, ending with a layer of mascarpone mixture on top.
  13. Dust the top with cocoa powder.
  14. Refrigerate for at least 4 hours, or overnight, to allow the flavors to meld together.
