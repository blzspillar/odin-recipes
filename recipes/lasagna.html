Lasagna

Description

This is a delicious and hearty lasagna recipe that's perfect for any occasion.

Ingredients

  • 1 pound ground beef
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 can (28 ounces) crushed tomatoes
  • 2 tablespoons tomato paste
  • 1 teaspoon dried basil
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 12 lasagna noodles
  • 16 ounces ricotta cheese
  • 2 cups shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. Cook the lasagna noodles according to the package instructions.
  3. In a large skillet, brown the ground beef and drain the excess fat.
  4. Add the onion and garlic, and cook until the onion is translucent.
  5. Stir in the crushed tomatoes, tomato paste, basil, oregano, salt, and pepper.
  6. Simmer for 15 minutes.
  7. In a mixing bowl, combine the ricotta cheese with 1 cup of mozzarella cheese.
  8. Spread a layer of meat sauce in the bottom of a 9x13 inch baking dish.
  9. Place a layer of noodles on top of the sauce.
  10. Spread half of the cheese mixture over the noodles.
  11. Repeat layers, ending with a layer of meat sauce.
  12. Top with the remaining mozzarella and Parmesan cheeses.
  13. Bake for 30 minutes or until the cheese is bubbly and golden brown.
